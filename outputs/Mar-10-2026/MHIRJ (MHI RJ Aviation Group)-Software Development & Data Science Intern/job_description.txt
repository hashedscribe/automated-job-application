About the job
MHIRJ (MHI RJ Aviation Group) is the fusion of two powerful legacies: Mitsubishi Heavy Industries (MHI) and the CRJ Series program. Headquartered in Boisbriand, Quebec, MHIRJ delivers comprehensive operational, engineering, and customer support solutions to the global regional aircraft industry. Our services include maintenance, refurbishment, technical publications, marketing, and sales activities.

With a strong Aerospace Engineering Center and a network of service centers, support offices, and parts depots across the world, we’re positioned to support our global customers wherever they fly.

From day one, you will work to provide insights and intelligent decision-making across functions and contribute to the delivery of real projects using the latest technology to solve business problems. You will receive one-on-one and group training and coaching from a team of talented experts and take on complex challenges in a rewarding environment.

The Sales & Operations Planning & Product Performance Team’s main objective rests with leveraging data and analytical techniques to guide business decisions with primary focus on the health of the company’s P&L. The team is divided between the Analytics and the Development team where you will get to work on various activities between both teams. Software Development & Data Science Interns are the direct source for providing insight to help enhance the fidelity of commercial decision making for the CRJ Aftermarket and help digitize that effort by developing Online tools & dashboards to deliver recommendations.
